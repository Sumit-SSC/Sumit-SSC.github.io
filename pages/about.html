Hi, I'm Sumit.

I turn messy data into clear, practical stories.

Welcome to my corner of the internet. I work at the intersection of analytics, product, and business—using SQL, Python, and BI tools to help teams ask better questions and make confident, data-informed decisions.

Learn More

What I Do

I’m an analytics professional who enjoys moving from raw tables to real outcomes. On most days you’ll find me defining the right metrics, digging through SQL, building Python workflows, and crafting BI dashboards that answer “so what?” for product and business teams. I care a lot about clarity, reproducible analysis, and making data easy to trust and act on.

Who I Am

I’m Sumit from Pune, India, originally trained as a Mechanical Engineer. Curiosity pulled me toward software, so I completed a PG Diploma in Computer Science and started my career as a software engineer working across backend, databases, and cloud. Along the way I realised the moments I enjoyed most were when numbers explained user behaviour or unlocked a business decision—so I leaned fully into analytics. Today I’m focused on analytics engineering, BI development, and decision science, and I’m actively looking for roles where I can combine technical depth with product thinking.
